# Exercise Plan

~235 lb · 5'11" · goal: lose fat + tighten chest, broaden arms & shoulders.
Gear: 25 lb + 11 lb dumbbell pairs, bench, bike + indoor cycle. ~1 hr/day · ~1700 cal · ~250 cal/day cardio.

> Lifting keeps the muscle so you look tight, not soft, as the fat comes off. Cardio adds to the deficit. Food does the actual fat loss. Skip rows is fine; don't skip protein.

---

## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Lift (~35 min) | Cardio (~20-25 min) |
|-----|------|--------|
| **Mon** | Strength A — Push | Moderate ride |
| **Tue** | Strength B — Pull | Hard intervals |
| **Wed** | Core circuit | Easy spin 20-30 min |
| **Thu** | Strength C — Chest & Shoulders | Moderate ride |
| **Fri** | Strength D — Arms & Core | Hard intervals |
| **Sat** | — | Long easy ride 40-60 min |
| **Sun** | Rest | Off (optional walk) |

Warm up first: 3-4 min easy spin + 1 light set. Rest ~45-60 sec between sets. Photos for every move are in **Exercise Demos** below.

### Strength A — Push (Mon)
- Incline Dumbbell Bench Press — 3 × 8-12
- Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press — 3 × 8-12
- Dumbbell Lateral Raise (11s) — 3 × 12-15
- Incline Dumbbell Fly — 3 × 10-15
- Goblet Squat — 3 × 12-15
- Bench Crunches — 3 × 15-20

### Strength B — Pull (Tue)
- One-Arm Dumbbell Row — 4 × 8-12 / side
- Seated Dumbbell Curl — 3 × 10-12
- Hammer Curl — 3 × 10-12
- Arnold Press — 3 × 8-12
- Reverse Lunge — 3 × 10 / leg
- Standing Calf Raise — 3 × 15-20

### Strength C — Chest & Shoulders (Thu)
- Flat Dumbbell Bench Press — 3 × 8-12
- Incline Dumbbell Fly — 3 × 10-15
- Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press — 3 × 8-12
- Dumbbell Lateral Raise (11s) — 3 × 12-15
- One-Arm Dumbbell Row — 3 × 10-12
- Dumbbell Squat — 3 × 10-15
- Leg Raises — 3 × 10-15

### Strength D — Arms & Core (Fri)
- Seated Dumbbell Curl — 3 × 10-12
- Single-Arm Dumbbell Curl — 3 × 10-12
- Close-Grip Dumbbell Press — 3 × 10
- Overhead Triceps Extension — 3 × 12
- Bench Sit-Ups — 3 × 15-20
- Sideways Sit-Ups — 3 × 12-15 / side

### Core circuit (Wed)
2-3 rounds, minimal rest: Bench Sit-Ups 15-20 · Leg Raises 10-15 · Sideways Sit-Ups 12-15/side · Plank 30-45 sec. Then 10 min stretch.

---

## Cheat Sheet

**Cardio** — Moderate: 25-30 min, talk-but-not-sing pace. Intervals: 30-60 sec hard / 90 sec easy × 8-10, with warm-up + cooldown. Long ride: 40-60 min easy.
**Food** — ~1700 cal, protein the priority at ~180-200g/day (meat, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, whey). Don't eat back cardio calories.
**Progress each week** — pick any one per lift: +1 rep · +1 set · 3-sec slow lower · 11s → 25s. When 25s feel easy at 12+ clean reps, switch to slow tempo + higher reps to keep it hard.
**Reality** — Weeks 1-2 feel rough and you'll be sore; just show up. Joint pain = stop; muscle soreness = fine. Take a baseline photo + weight today; photos tell the truth, the scale lies week to week.
