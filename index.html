Cheat Sheet

Weight rule: pick a load you can do at the bottom of the rep range but struggle near the top. Hit the top on all sets, then add a rep, a set, slow the lower to 3 sec, or move 11s to 25s.

Rest: ~45-60 sec between sets to keep the heart rate up for fat loss.

Cardio: Moderate = talk-but-not-sing pace. Intervals = 30-60s hard / 90s easy x 8-10. Don't go hard every day.

Food: ~1700 cal, protein first at ~180-200g/day. Don't eat back cardio calories.

Reality: Weeks 1-2 feel rough, just show up. Joint pain = stop; muscle soreness = fine. Take a baseline photo today.

Customize
